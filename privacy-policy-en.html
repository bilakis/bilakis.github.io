Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2025

Overview

WikiLinks is a game where you navigate to a target article by following Wikipedia links. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data.

Data We Collect

  • Anonymous User ID: Automatically generated for use in in-game leaderboards. It is not linked to your real identity.
  • Username: An optional nickname you choose, displayed on leaderboards.
  • Game Statistics: Click counts, elapsed time, and game results.

How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used solely to provide the game experience (leaderboards, daily challenges, play with friends). Your data is not shared with third parties or used for advertising purposes.

Data Storage

Game data is securely stored on Supabase infrastructure. Only your preferences (language, username) are stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

  • Wikipedia API: Article content is fetched from Wikipedia. Wikipedia's own privacy policy applies.
  • Supabase: Used for anonymous authentication and data storage.
  • Google AdMob: Used to display ads. Google's privacy policy applies. You can remove ads via the in-app purchase.

Children's Privacy

WikiLinks does not collect personal information. The anonymous user ID is generated automatically and cannot be linked to a real identity. The app is suitable for all age groups.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at bilakis.app@gmail.com.
